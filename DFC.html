How fare is calculated

  • Taxi: ₹40 base + (distance - 1 km) × (₹20 AC / ₹17 Non-AC)
  • Auto: ₹30 base + (distance - 1.5 km) × ₹11
  • Night: 25% extra on subtotal
  • Waiting: ₹1/min (Taxi) or ₹0.75/min (Auto)
  • Luggage: ₹15 (Taxi) or ₹10 (Auto) per piece
